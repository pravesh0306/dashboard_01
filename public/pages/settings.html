Canva Integration

Enter the URL of your default Canva template for new orders.

How to link Canva designs:

  1. Go to Canva and open or create your design
  2. Click "Share" in the top right corner
  3. Set permissions to "View" or "Edit" as needed
  4. Copy the link and paste it in the order's Canva URL field
